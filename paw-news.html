Pawtown Mayor Shushi set to take her first speech after becoming a mayor

Shushi the cat, who won the mayor elections by a huge margin of votes, is set to give her first speech. Her speech will be held on 15th June 2023 at the Amphitheatre. Shushi has exclaimed that she feels very happy about being a mayor and will do everything to make the town progress into a better future. To begin with, she said that all the attendees of the speech will get free canned tuna. Yum!
